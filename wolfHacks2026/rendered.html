About Us

Why We Built ER Watch

Having seen firsthand the uncertainty and stress of ER waiting rooms, we aimed to build a tool that provides clarity when you need it most.

Built with Purpose

ER Watch was developed to address the challenges faced by patients waiting in emergency rooms. We provide real-time information to help patients make informed decisions in genuine emergencies.

Community Focused

Our platform serves to inform those with urgent medical needs, helping them make quicker decisions when facing a medical emergency. We believe in transparency and accessibility for all.

Driving Impact

By providing live wait times, we help decrease ER overcrowding and ensure timely care for patients with serious injuries or illnesses. Consider urgent care for non-emergency issues.
